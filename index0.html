Interests

My research aim is to apply High Performance Computing (HPC) to Machine Learning (ML), expand its application, and study its advancement. In recent years, ML has become an important tool for solving various problems in Physics, medical field, bio informatics, image processing, and artificial intelligence and so forth. However, as the amount of learning data increases and the number of features increases, it will reach a limit such that it shall take a considerable amount of time to obtain results in a conventional computing environment. HPC, by its nature, can handle large amounts of floating-point computations in parallel, and has very little latency in I/O, therefore future ML will increasingly require the use of HPC.
